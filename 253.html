REFORM YOUR SELF

by Dr. Mohsin Ali

Ask yourself and your children whether they just dream or do something like the Companions of the Prophet (peace be upon him), called Sahabah. Dreamers pass life just thinking. I often suggest to my children these things: remember Allah in silence and calmness, remain focused towards duties and responsibilities, then perform them through Sunnah; this way neither are neglected, practice self control, so that you don't commit any evil. This world is a place of deception, displeasures and filled with Satan's trap so have knowledge and patience to counter them. (Quran 3:185, Sura Ali-Imran)

Surah Ali-Imran states 'the life of this world is no more than the delusion of enjoyment.'
