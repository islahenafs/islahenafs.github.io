ISLAHE NAFS
REFORM YOUR SELF

by Dr. Mohsin Ali

This is the supplication which archangel Gabriel narrated and Prophet (PBUH) said Ameen during Ramzan:

That man is doomed who spends his Ramadan and his evil deeds are not forgiven.

That man is doomed who does not offer Durood when Prophet's sws name is recited/ discussed in front of him

That man is doomed whose parents are alive and he doesn't earn the paradise by serving them





ISLAHE NAFS
